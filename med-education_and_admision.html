Welcome Applicants!

Thank you for your interest in our School of Medicine. Prospective students will be primarily drawn from a national pool of applicants with a focus on disadvantaged California and Inland Empire resident. The MD program at the California University of Science and Medicine offers a system-based curriculum where the basic science disciplines are taught in the context of clinical presentations. This allows the student to see first hand how the basic sciences are linked to the practice of medicine.

To this end, candidates who have keen observational skills, are logical, possess comprehensive analytical abilities, compassion, integrity, and good interpersonal skills are strongly encouraged to apply. In order to assess the personal qualities of applicants, CalMed utilizes a holistic review system in the admission review process.
